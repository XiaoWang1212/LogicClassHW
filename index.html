Flexible Engagement Models

Choose the cooperation model that best suits your business needs

Fixed Price

Ideal for projects with well-defined requirements and clear deliverables. We agree on a fixed cost and timeline upfront.

Best for:
  • Small to medium-sized projects
  • Clear specifications
  • Defined timeline
  • Budget certainty

Time & Material

Flexible approach where you pay for the actual time spent on your project, allowing for changes and adjustments as needed.

Best for:
  • Evolving requirements
  • Ongoing development
  • Projects needing flexibility
  • Agile methodology

Dedicated Team

A team of specialists working exclusively on your project, fully integrated with your processes and communication channels.
